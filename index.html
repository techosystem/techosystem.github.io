BIO

I am currently pursuing a PhD in Computer Science at the University of Maryland, Baltimore County (UMBC), under the guidance of Prof. Karuna Joshi at the Knowledge, Analytics, Cognitive and Cloud Computing (KnACC) lab.

My primary area of research interest is Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, focusing on combining computer vision with natural language processing. Currently, I'm working on developing an algorithm to enhance medical image segmentation using these technologies.

With extensive experience in both industry and academia, I have honed my skills as a Data Scientist at Meteolytix GmbH in Germany and as an IT Analyst at Enugu State University of Science and Technology. My academic qualifications include a Master of Science in Computer Science from the University of Lagos, a Master of Science in Data Science from Kiel University of Applied Sciences, and a Bachelor of Engineering from Enugu State University of Science and Technology, Enugu.
